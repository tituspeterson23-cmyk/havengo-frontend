About HavenGo Uganda

HavenGo is Uganda's leading on-demand home services platform, connecting trusted providers with customers across Kampala, Wakiso, and Entebbe.

Founded in 2024, we have grown from a small startup into a trusted marketplace serving thousands of happy customers. Our mission is to make home services accessible, reliable, and affordable for every Ugandan household.
